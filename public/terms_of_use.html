We’re Here to Help You Every Step of the Way

Welcome to TableTick. Protecting your personal information is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website, app, and services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

    a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you sign up, make purchases, or contact support.
  • Account Information: Login credentials, preferences, and any content you upload to the platform.

    b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on our platform, and actions taken (e.g., placing an order or processing payments).
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: To improve functionality and analyze site traffic.

    c. Third-Party Information

  • Data from third-party integrations or services (e.g., payment processors or social media logins).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services.
  • To process transactions and communicate order details.
  • To improve functionality, personalize your experience, and optimize our platform.
  • To send important updates, such as changes to policies or service disruptions.
  • To conduct security checks and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Your data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccuracies in your information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is used under certain conditions.
  • Withdraw Consent: For data processing based on consent, you may withdraw at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@tabletick.com.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and regular monitoring. However, no system is entirely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or comply with legal obligations.

Cookies And Tracking Technologies

Our website and app use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience.
  • Analyze traffic and performance.
  • Deliver personalized ads and content.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third Party Links

Our platform may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

Children’s Privacy

TableTick is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review it regularly. Significant updates will be communicated via email or notices on our platform.
